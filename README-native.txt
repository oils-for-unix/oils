Oils for Unix
=============

Oils runs in 2 modes:

- OSH runs your existing shell scripts
- YSH is the ultimate glue language, which is like shell, Python, JSON, and
  YAML, seamlessly put together.

It's our upgrade path from bash to a better language and runtime!
